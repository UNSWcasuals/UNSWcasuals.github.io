About

The UNSW Casuals Network is a grassroots organisation of casualised and insecure staff at UNSW, fighting for job security and better working conditions.

In 2020, we’ve written about how we think university workers need to organise in our interests, conducted a workers’ inquiry to document the experience of UNSW casual workers in the early phase of Australia’s pandemic response, and participated together in protests on and off campus.

You can contact us via our Facebook page, Twitter, or email.
